MEATBALLS

Delicious Home Make meatballs

Today we are going to teach you how to make homemade meatballs from scratch. Hopefully you are a big fan of bold flavors and lots of garlic.

We will be making our own break crumbs from scratch, using only fresh herbs and free range eggs in this recipe. below is a list of ingredients that will be needed for the recipe

Ingredients List

  • 1 lbs 80/20 ground beef
  • 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • Fresh Oregano
  • Fresh Basil
  • Fresh Tyme
  • salt and pepper
  • Jalapeno if you like some heat
