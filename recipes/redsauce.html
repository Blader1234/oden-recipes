Red Sauce

Lets make some homemade tomato sauce to dip your garlic bread into or serve over your favorite pasta.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans hole peeled tomato's
  • 1 small can tomato paste
  • 6 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 medium yellow onion (chopped)
  • oregano
  • basil
  • tyme
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • teaspoon sugar
  • salt and pepper
  • a hot pepper of your choosing (chopped)(optional)

Instructions

  1. chop up the tomato's from the can
  2. chop up the oregano, basil and tyme
  3. pre-heat a sauce pan and add olive oil to it
  4. saute the onions and garlic until translucent
  5. add chopped tomato's, paste and herbs
  6. add sugar
  7. add salt and pepper to taste
  8. cook on high heat until sauce starts to boil
  9. turn down heat to low and let simmer for 3 hours (stirring several time an hour)
